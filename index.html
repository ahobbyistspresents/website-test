About AHP

Design, data, and fun drive every product.

Eoghan McHugh, a cycling and guitar enthusiast, started ACP in 2023 and AGP in 2024 to create giftware for cyclists and guitarists.

Each product answers four key questions: Is it clear? Is the design and quality gift-worthy? Will the recipient enjoy it? Will they get something from it? Every item is crafted with these principles, ensuring thoughtful, well-designed gifts for cyclists and guitarists alike.
